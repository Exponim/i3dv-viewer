progress()

This just updates the loading bar and then calls loadComplete when the percentage loaded is greater than 99%. In the case of Chrome, it also checks to see if the percentage is changing. Chrome has a problem with buffering videos in that it's buffer cache is *very* small (less than 3mb) hence it needs to be initiated before the video actually finishes loading. It will store the whole video once it starts playing however, but this does mean there might be a bit of lag on Chrome.
